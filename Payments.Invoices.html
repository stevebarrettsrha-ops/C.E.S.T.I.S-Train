How the documents connect

Cashbook → Payment Vouchers. Enter transactions on the Cashbook page as usual; every payment automatically gets a voucher here. A transaction with a cheque number produces a Cheque Payment Voucher; one without a cheque number is treated as a bank transfer and produces a Bank Transfer Voucher. Deposits and cancelled cheques are skipped.
Quote → Invoice. When a client accepts a quote, open it and press “Convert to Invoice”.
All documents are numbered automatically, saved on this device (and included in cloud backups of browser data), and print on letter/A4 paper ready for signatures and the office stamp.
